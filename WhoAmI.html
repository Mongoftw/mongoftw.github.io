    FRC

    FRC stands for First Robotics Competition which is put on by FIRST. This program is for students from when they start school to when they leave high school. They can participate in four different types of competitions: JRFLL, FLL, FTC, FRC. Each are different competitions based on grade level for the students. They can start out with legos in elementary school to full blown robots in high school. This was a program that came to my school when I was a second year. I got to join FRC and build robots to compete in games designed by FIRST. This program helped me build my leadership skills which I didn't know that I had before. Without this program, I probably wouldn't be at Michigan Tech and I'm really happy I got to experience FRC. If you would like to check out FRC you can go to their site by clicking here .
